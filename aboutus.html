Our Story

After being attendees of conferences ourselves and struggling with paper copies of conference information, we felt there had to be a better way for us to browse and organize everything...So we developed a mobile application to improve the conference-going experience!


Our Mission

Your organization probably already has some solutions helping you move away from paper and manual methods of managing your membership, events, and data, but we want to help you streamline your process to save you time and headache while making your organization as effective as possible.

Our goal is to enhance your events while giving you control over all aspects of your organization, be it your membership, programs, sponsors, and more. Our top priority is great service and helping you succeed.
